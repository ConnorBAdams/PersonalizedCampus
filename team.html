The Drive Behind the Mission

With the sudden situation of a global pandemic, traveling has been greatly reduced. Our goal originally was to create a tour application for the primary use of campuses who were not receiving the usual amount of prospective students roaming their buildings and greenery. While working on the application, we decided to open the possibility to everyday users. Now, anyone can create tours for any place in the world and share them with their friends who have the option of taking the tour either virtually from the comfort of their own homes, or in person. There also exists the option of taking tours that have been posted to the public from verified users. So if a person were to visit a city, they can look for tours that have been posted and take them as they please.
